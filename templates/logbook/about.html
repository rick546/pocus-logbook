What is manitobapocus.pythonanywhere.com?

manitobapocus.pythonanywhere.com is an educational portal dedicated to Point-of-Care Ultrasound (POCUS) training. It brings together hands-on scanning protocols, interactive clinical cases, knowledge-check quizzes, curated learning resources, and a personal scan logbook all in one place.

The platform is designed to support learners as they build competency in POCUS, letting them track their progress, review core protocols, test their understanding, and document the scans they perform throughout their training.

About the Creator

This portal was created by a resident physician in the CCFP-EM (Emergency Medicine) program at the University of Winnipeg. It was developed as part of his scholarly project, with the goal of building a practical, accessible resource to advance POCUS education for residents and learners. The project reflects a commitment to improving ultrasound training through structured, self-directed learning tools.
